  • Ngebel Lake

    Ngebel Lake is a natural lake located in Ngebel District, Ponorogo Regency. Ngebel District is located on the slopes of Mount Wilis. Telaga Ngebel is located about 30 km from downtown Ponorogo with an altitude of 734 meters above sea level. The circumference of Ngebel Lake is about 5 km and the temperature in this lake ranges from 20°C to 26°C.

  • Pletuk Waterfall

    Pletuk Waterfall or also known as Coban Temu is a waterfall located in Jurug Village, Sooko District, or more precisely south of Pulung District. This waterfall has a height of about 30 meters with an altitude of 450 meters above the sea. This area is surrounded by towering hills and overgrown with a number of plants.

  • Lowo Cave

    Lowo Cave is located in Sampung District, about 20 km from the city center of Ponorogo. This waterfall is called Lowo Cave because it is inhabited by many bats. Bats that live in this cave is free and does not disturb the local community. In this cave also found archaeological sites that have high archaeological value. The environment around this cave is very natural and surrounded by trees and rocks.

  • Ngembag Tourism Park

    Ngembag Tourism Park is a tourist park located in Ronowijayan Village, Siman District. This park consists of a water source equipped with a playground and children's pool. Previously, Ngembag was known as an untreated spring. Then it was converted into a city park equipped with a children's pool and also some children's games by the Ponorogo Regency Government.
